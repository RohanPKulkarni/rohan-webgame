ABOUT THE GAME

Welcome to Rohan's Dragon Ball Z game.This is a simple one on one game designed by using web development fundamentals, including an understanding of HTML (Hypertext Markup Language) , CSS (Cascading Style Sheets) and JavaScript.

HOW TO PLAY

The game starts with 0 charges for either players.Charge is the fuel required to attack the opponent. Game consists 6 attacking moves and 5 defensive moves.The 6 attacking moves (with required number of charges) are Kamehameha(1),BeamCannon(2),DestructoDisc(3),GalickGun(4) ,FinalFlash(5) and Sphere of Distruction(6).Defensive moves require no charges.Every attack has only 1 defense.EnergyShield is the defense against Kam,BeamShield is the defense against BeamCannon, Teleport against DestructoDisc,FlashProtection against FinalFlash and SuperVest against GalickGun.There is no defense against Sphere of Distruction.Attacking move with higher number of charge requirement beats the attacking move with lower number of charge requirement.If number of charges of any player goes below 0,the player loses.
